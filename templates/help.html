Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Greenfield Hub and how we support our local producers.

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Browse our Catalogue, select the products you want, and click "Add to Order". Once you're ready, your order is processed directly by the producers.

Where do the products come from?

All our products are sourced directly from local farmers and artisans within a 50-mile radius of Greenfield. Check out our Producers page to meet the people behind your food.

Is the packaging eco-friendly?

Yes! We prioritize sustainable practices. Most of our producers use compostable or recyclable packaging to minimize our environmental footprint.

How does delivery work?

We offer weekly delivery slots every Tuesday and Friday. You can select your preferred delivery time at checkout. We also have several pickup points around the city.

Can I sell my products here?

We are always looking for new local producers! If you're a farmer or maker in the Greenfield area, please contact us at partners@greenfieldhub.com to start the application process.
